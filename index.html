Who is Artica?

Artica is a rock band from Lexington, KY. Formed in 2016, the band had great success locally and regionally with their debut EP "In Honesty" that they released to fans for no cost at all. After playing dozens of shows in different states and distributing their unique blend of mid-2000s Post-Hardcore with modern Alternative Rock influences, Artica went into the studio in 2017 with legendary producer Matt Goldman (Underoath, Anberlin, The Devil Wears Prada) to create what became their sophomore EP, "Color Restored".

Boasting four high powered tracks that cut straight to the point, Artica is again ready to tour the country and break out as the next big thing of 2018. "Color Restored" was announced with a dynamic and visually pleasing music video for the lead-off single, "Cloak & Claw" at the beginning of the year. The EP releases on 3/16/18.
